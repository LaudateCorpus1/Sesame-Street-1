Coldplay

Favorite concert at Lollapalooza 2011! Jane and I were there and thought we'd leave to go see Girl Talk and others and then make it back in time for the finale. Thank goodness we didn't follow through with our plan.

Coldplay played after Bright Eyes and when the crowd dissipated we pushed forward to almost the very front. When I say incredible, I mean incredible. We had no idea what to expect and I think that made it all the better.

Chris Martin is our hero. We met him after The Secret Policeman's Ball at Radio City Music Hall in New York, but that will come later.
