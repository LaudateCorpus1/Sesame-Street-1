The Cults

Gilt City Chicago put on this totally rad roof top party during Lollapalooza 2011. It was such a scene. Good food, free drinks, and most importantly really awesome band.

I had actually never heard The Cults before and before they started playing they were just hanging out at the party so they just blended into the party. It started raining during their set and the just kept playing saying that as long as we stayed to listen they would keep going. Dedication.
