The Black Keys

The Black Keys is a rock band that's been around for 10+ years but only recently has BLOWN up big time. Dan Auerbach and Patrick Carney, the founding members, were childhood friends who started the band out of a garage in their hometown of Akron, Ohio.

At Coachella 2012 they played directly following PULP on the Mainstage. It was a killer show and they even brought out John Fogerty to play a song with them, which was epic. This was a tribute to the late Levon Helm of The Band and they played a version of "The Weight," a song from The Band.Here's a recording featured in Rolling Stone, enjoy!

My friend had tried to get me to go their show at Madison Square Garden the week before, and I'm so glad I waited because I honestly had no idea what to expect (I obviously knew I loved the band), but never imagined their show would be so incredible. My two favorites were definitely Howlin' For You and Lonely Boy hands down!
